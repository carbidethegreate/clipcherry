Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I reset my password?

    Go to the login page and click on “Forgot Password”; we will send a reset link to your email.

  • How do I become a creator?

    Visit the Creator Portal, enter your user ID and follow the prompts to activate creator mode.

  • Where can I check my age verification status?

    After signing up, visit the Verification Status page (linked from your dashboard) to see whether your ID has been verified.

  • How are payments handled?

    Payments are processed directly on the blockchain; we never store your funds and unlock content once your transaction is confirmed.
